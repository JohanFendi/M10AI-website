About M10AI

Origin

It all began over lunch at Gothenburg University. Alejandro and Shariq, two students passionate about AI, envisioned a space for students to explore and innovate in AI. That vision became M10 AI, a student-led association. Since its founding, M10 AI has hosted workshops on machine learning and explored topics like blockchain, growing into a hub for AI enthusiasts.

What we do

The AI Student Organization at the University of Gothenburg empowers students to explore and innovate in artificial intelligence. Through workshops, hackathons, and industry connections, it fosters collaboration, skill development, and real-world problem-solving in AI.
